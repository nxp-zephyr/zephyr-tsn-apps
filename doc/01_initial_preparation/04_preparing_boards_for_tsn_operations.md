#  Preparing boards for TSN operations

## i.MX RT1180 EVK board

For TSN Bridge operation 4 ports of the i.MX RT1180 Ethernet switch are available, namely ENET0 (100Mbps port) and ENET1, ENET2 and ENET3 (1Gbps ports).

Make sure that i.MX RT1180 EVK board matches USE CASE1 option from schematic SCH-50577 rev C2 page 31 to enable 4 ethernet ports.
